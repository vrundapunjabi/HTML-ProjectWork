Resort

What we offer

Outdoor Pool

A great family pool in beautiful Borden Park, it is surrounded by sand and plenty of lounge space with umbrellas, making it a perfect destination to enjoy a summer day. As no chemicals are used to disinfect the water, the guidelines of the pool are different from other outdoor pools. Please read the pool guidelines before visiting the facility.

Indoor Pool

The pool is inside the resort and you can enjoy and relax in the swimming pool.There are two different areas for children and other for the family.You can relax and rest in the pool.

Spa Zone

At Spa Zone we offer esthetic services for men, women and children.Our Services Include: Dermalogica facials,skin care treatments, manicure, pedicure, full body waxing, male waxing, eyelash tinting, eyebrow tinting, eyelash extension, shellac manicures and shellac pedicures.Introducing the MED ZONE: Laser Hair Removal, Skin Tightening, Face/Body Contour, Laser assisted Skin Rejuvenation, and Electrolysis!!

Sports Area

Marimar hotel & Resorts welcomes dozens of hockey, baseball, basketball, soccer and other teams every year: clear proof of our outstanding service. You too will enjoy the service we provide.

Restaurant

The Resort also gives the facility of Restuarant where you can have different kind of foods you want.You can just hangout with your families and friends in the restuarant

Skybar

SkyBar is the place to enjoy unforgettable views of the city lights, signature cocktails created by expert mixologists, local and craft beers, wines, spirits, and light fare.
